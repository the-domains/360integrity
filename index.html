I MUST Lead.

UP! UP!

I MUST be UP

Following others? Easy. Taking the lead? Cover me please.

EQUAL = EQUITY! ... Right?

The tyranny in...

always needing to lead,

always following, or

insisting on pervasive equality

prevents trust.

Being adept and fluid as a leader, a follower and a peer

instills trust.

Always feeling mighty? It's sometimes wise to step aside.

Enjoy another's lead for awhile.

And, know when to soar ahead!

Sometimes she's the boss. Sometimes it has to be me.

We're playfully equal.
